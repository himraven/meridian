## SEO Strategy Per Article

Every article JSON includes:
- `seo.keywords` ‚Äî 5-8 long-tail keywords
- `seo.description` ‚Äî 150-160 char meta description
- `content_md` ‚Äî H2/H3 structure, FAQ sections for featured snippets
- `hero_stat` ‚Äî Data point for social sharing
- `social.hook_en` / `social.hook_zh` ‚Äî Pre-written social copy
- `related_articles` ‚Äî Internal linking to other L1/L2/L3

## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- Every article starts with clear TL;DR
- FAQ-style H2 headings ("Why do congress members outperform?")
- Specific data points with sources (citation-friendly)
- Structured data (Article + FAQ schema) in frontend
